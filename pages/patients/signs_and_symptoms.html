Signs & Symptoms of Diabetes

Patients with Diabetes can experience different signs and symptoms.

However symptoms may be absent especially in those with type 2 diabetes.

These symptoms and signs include the following:

  • Polyurea (Frequent urination)
  • Polydipsia (Excessive thirst)
  • Polyphagia (Increased hunger)
  • Weight loss
  • Tiredness and lethargy
  • Blurred vision
  • Frequent infections
  • Slow-healing wounds

If i have signs, what should i do?

See your doctor so he or she can perform tests to determine whether or not you have diabetes or prediabetes.
Your doctor will make recommendations to bring down your blood sugar level to treat diabetes or so that you can
delay the development of diabetes.
